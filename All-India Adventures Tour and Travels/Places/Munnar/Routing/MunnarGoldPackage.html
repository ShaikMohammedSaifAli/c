Details Of The GOLD Package



    Day 1: Arrival in Munnar


    🌿 Welcome to Munnar, a picturesque hill station nestled amidst lush greenery and tea gardens! Upon your arrival at Cochin International Airport or Ernakulam Railway Station, our friendly team will greet you and transfer you to your cozy accommodation in Munnar.


    Day 2: Munnar Exploration


    🌄 Rise and shine, nature lovers! Today, we embark on a full-day exploration of Munnar's breathtaking landscapes and attractions. After a hearty breakfast, we set off to discover the wonders of this enchanting hill station.


    🌹 Our first stop is Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and lush greenery as far as the eye can see. Next, we visit the iconic Mattupetty Dam, offering stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys.


    🏞️ We then proceed to Top Station, where you can marvel at panoramic vistas of the Western Ghats and enjoy the cool mountain breeze. Later, we visit Echo Point, where the scenic beauty is enhanced by the natural echo phenomenon.


    🚣‍♂️ Our day concludes with a visit to Kundala Lake, where you can enjoy a leisurely boat ride amidst serene surroundings. After a day filled with exploration and adventure, return to your accommodation for a peaceful evening.


    Day 3: Waterfalls and Wildlife


    🌳 Good morning, wildlife enthusiasts! Today, we delve into the natural wonders of Munnar, starting with a visit to the majestic Attukal Waterfalls. Feel the mist on your face as you marvel at the cascading waters amidst lush greenery.


    🦌 Next, we embark on an excursion to Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary, home to a diverse array of flora and fauna, including the endangered grizzled giant squirrel and the Indian star tortoise. Explore the sanctuary on a guided trek and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature.


    🌿 As the day draws to a close, we return to Munnar and visit the scenic Pothamedu View Point, offering panoramic views of tea estates and mist-covered hills. Spend the evening relaxing and reflecting on the day's adventures.


    Day 4: Anamudi Peak Expedition


    🏔️ Wake up to the allure of adventure as we embark on an exhilarating trek to Anamudi Peak, the highest point in South India. After a hearty breakfast, we begin our journey to the base of the peak, surrounded by verdant forests and meandering streams.


    🥾 Lace up your hiking boots and set off on a memorable trek through the wilderness, taking in the sights and sounds of the pristine surroundings. As you ascend to the summit of Anamudi Peak, be rewarded with breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape stretching as far as the eye can see.


    🌅 After soaking in the awe-inspiring vistas, we descend from the peak and return to Munnar, where you can spend the rest of the day at leisure, indulging in relaxation or exploring the local markets.


    Day 5: Departure


    🚗 Alas, it's time to bid farewell to the serene beauty of Munnar. After a leisurely breakfast, our team will assist you with check-out from your accommodation.


    🛣️ We'll then transfer you to Cochin International Airport or Ernakulam Railway Station for your onward journey, filled with cherished memories of your time amidst the tea gardens, waterfalls, and wildlife of Munnar. Safe travels, and until we meet again!
